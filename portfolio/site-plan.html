Purpose

This is a portfolio showcasing that I am an intuitive cybersecurity expert who is keen in providing infrastructure that will assure you in a place of comfort, amidst a world of hackers who are looking to hold you valuable assets hostage. My website will showcase that I excel in computer programming and that I'm excellent in applying information technology to any environment. My intended audience are both hiring managers and cybersecurity administrators, working in the fields of healthcare, clinics, and hospitals.
