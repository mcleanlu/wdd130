Overview

Purpose

This is a portfolio showcasing that I am an intuitive cybersecurity expert who is keen in providing infrastructure that will assure you in a place of comfort, amidst a world of hackers who are looking to hold you valuable assets hostage. My website will showcase that I excel in computer programming and that I'm excellent in applying information technology to any environment. This site will also show that I am well versed in the world of corporation and office culture, but I also value the hard grit of the working class professional and the spirit of growth in a company.

Audience

My intended audience are both hiring managers and cybersecurity administrators, working in the fields of healthcare, clinics, and hospitals. I am also showcasing myself to corporate management who are potentially working with fortune 500 companies who are concerned about the changing technical environment and how it will affect their business ventures.
